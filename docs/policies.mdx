### Refund Policy

We do not usually offer refunds for API usage. If you believe your API keys were compromised, reach out to [support@relace.ai](mailto:support@relace.ai) for help remediating the problem.

### Subscription Cancellation

You may cancel your subscription at any time. Upon cancellation, you will instantly lose access to any Relace models accessible through the API.
